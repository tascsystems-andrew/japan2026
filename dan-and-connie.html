Days 1–4

Arrive & Discover Osaka

Your first days are about settling in and soaking up Osaka — Japan's kitchen. We'll show you the castle, the neon-lit food streets of Dotonbori, and the retro charm of Shinsekai. Then you'll have a self-guided day to explore on your own before we head to Kyoto together for the thousand torii gates of Fushimi Inari and the geisha district of Gion.

Mon Nov 9

Osaka Day Tour

Osaka Castle, Kuromon Market for lunch, Shinsekai district. Your introduction to the city that never stops eating.

Wed Nov 11

Kyoto Day Tour

Fushimi Inari's thousand orange torii gates, Kiyomizu-dera temple, the charming lanes of Ninenzaka, and an evening in Gion.

Days 5–9

Kobe & Himeji

The centrepiece of your trip. A bullet train to Kobe — a stylish harbour city wedged between mountains and sea. Two nights exploring Chinatown, the waterfront, and the world-famous beef. Then a morning train to Himeji for Japan's most breathtaking castle, and a day trip to the ancient hot spring town of Arima before heading home.

Thu Nov 12

Shinkansen to Kobe

Your bullet train moment. Shin-Osaka to Shin-Kobe in 13 minutes at 280 km/h. Afternoon in Kitano Ijinkan foreign houses district.

Sat Nov 14

Himeji Castle

The White Heron Castle — UNESCO World Heritage Site, one of 12 original Japanese castles. Five stories, stunning views, and genuinely awe-inspiring architecture. Just 30 minutes from Kobe by train.

Sun Nov 15

Arima Onsen Day Trip

A charming 1,000-year-old hot spring town tucked in the mountains just 30 minutes from Kobe. Soak in the famous golden waters at the public bathhouses and stroll the quaint streets.

Days 10–15

Second Week Highlights

Universal Studios Japan with the whole crew, Sam's birthday Kobe beef teppanyaki dinner with all nine of us around the grill, a trip to the original CUPNOODLES Museum to make your own custom ramen, a day trip to the serene tea town of Uji, and a combined farewell and birthday dinner for Adrienne. The pace eases off — these days are about enjoying each other's company.

Wed Nov 18

Universal Studios Japan

Nintendo World, Harry Potter, and all the rides. USJ is just a 10-minute train ride from our front door. Full day of fun for everyone.

Fri Nov 20

CUPNOODLES Museum

The birthplace of instant ramen! Head to Ikeda (30 min train) to visit the original museum where Momofuku Ando invented Cup Noodle. Design your own custom cup, pick your toppings, and take it home. The kids will be talking about this one for weeks.

Sat Nov 21

Uji, Farewell & Adrienne's Birthday

A peaceful tea town with Byodo-in Temple (it's on the 10-yen coin!), matcha ice cream, and a river walk. Back to Osaka for a farewell dinner for you and a birthday celebration for Adrienne.
