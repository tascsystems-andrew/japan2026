Optional Itinerary: Mt. Fuji

Swap Kobe Weekend for Mt. Fuji

Experience Japan's iconic Mt. Fuji region — combining the natural beauty of onsen, ryokan stays, and the cultural vibrancy of Kawaguchiko — as an alternative to the Kobe weekend. This plan offers stunning views of the mountain, relaxation, and a different take on Japanese charm.

  • Day 1 (Thu Nov 12): Travel by Shinkansen to Mishima. Transfer to Kawaguchiko. Evening at a ryokan and hot spring baths.
  • Day 2 (Fri Nov 13): Morning exploring Lake Kawaguchi. Optional outlet shopping. Afternoon train to Kanazawa, exploring the Higashi Chaya tea districts.
  • Day 3 (Sat Nov 13): Visiting Kenroku-en Garden, Kanazawa Castle, and departure back to Osaka.
