Welcome

Days 1–4

Arrive & Discover Osaka

Your first days are about settling in and soaking up Osaka — Japan's kitchen. We'll show you the castle, the neon-lit food streets of Dotonbori, and the retro charm of Shinsekai. Then you'll have a self-guided day to explore on your own before we head to Kyoto together for the thousand torii gates of Fushimi Inari and the geisha district of Gion.

Mon Nov 9

Osaka Day Tour

Osaka Castle, Kuromon Market for lunch, Shinsekai district. Your introduction to the city that never stops eating.

Wed Nov 11

Kyoto Day Tour

Fushimi Inari's thousand orange torii gates, Kiyomizu-dera temple, the charming lanes of Ninenzaka, and an evening in Gion.

Days 5–9

The Hyogo Circuit

The centrepiece of your trip. A bullet train to Kobe, two nights exploring the harbour city, then north by limited express to Kinosaki Onsen — a 1,400-year-old hot spring town where you'll stroll the lantern-lit streets in yukata and wooden sandals, soak in seven different bathhouses, and feast on snow crab. Then south to Himeji for Japan's most breathtaking castle before heading home.

Thu Nov 12

Shinkansen to Kobe

Your bullet train moment. Shin-Osaka to Shin-Kobe in 13 minutes at 280 km/h. Afternoon in Kitano Ijinkan foreign houses district.

Sat Nov 14

Kinosaki Onsen

A charming hot spring town in the mountains. Change into yukata, grab your onsen pass, and hop between seven public bathhouses. Evening snow crab kaiseki dinner at the ryokan.

Sun Nov 15

Himeji Castle

The White Heron Castle — UNESCO World Heritage Site, one of 12 original Japanese castles. Five stories, stunning views, and genuinely awe-inspiring architecture.

Days 10–15

Second Week Highlights

Universal Studios Japan with the whole crew, Sam's birthday Kobe beef teppanyaki dinner with all nine of us around the grill, a day trip to the serene tea town of Uji, and a farewell dinner together. The pace eases off — these days are about enjoying each other's company.

Wed Nov 18

Universal Studios Japan

Nintendo World, Harry Potter, and all the rides. USJ is just a 10-minute train ride from our front door. Full day of fun for everyone.

Sat Nov 21

Uji, Farewell & Adrienne's Birthday

A peaceful tea town with Byodo-in Temple (it's on the 10-yen coin!), matcha ice cream, and a river walk. Back to Osaka for a combined farewell dinner for Dan & Connie and Adrienne's birthday celebration.
